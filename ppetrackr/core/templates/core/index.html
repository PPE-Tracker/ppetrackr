Track Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) across critical healthcare infrastructures

Using PPETrackr, healthcare facilities can log their daily use of PPE and view different visualizations of their data in the dashboard. This data can also be centralized with other healthcare facilities' data to depict PPE trends across the region. Government leaders can then make efficient and effective decisions about the acquisition, allocation, and conservation of PPE supplies.
